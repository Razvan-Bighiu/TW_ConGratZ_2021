Abstract

ConGratZ is a website based on a greeting card/business card sharing model, where users can create and share their business card or greeting card templates for other users in order to spread the love and good vibes. :) This documentation describes the last version of the website and things that we implemented.

Introduction

As we all know, these days, the physical interaction is a more delicate subject. Back in the day, people had to go in certain places to share their feelings to people that they love or admire, but now we can do that from our computer and I know, it's not the same thing, but the application does it's job to meet our needs.

The purpose of this website is to:

  • create, download & share greeting card templates and business cards

Overview

The Technologies used

The entire Web Application is powered by PHP & JS.

MySQL will be used for the data storage.

The Product Perspective

ConGratZ is a stand-alone website.

The Product Functions

  • Create and publish Greeting Cards and Templates.
  • Create and send Business Cards for your small business.
  • Search for User-made Card Templates

    • User Classes and Characteristics

    There are three major user classes:

  • Firstly, business people who want to create cards for their business and can't hire a professional designer.
  • Second user class would be Middle aged men and women who want to send a greeting card to their relatives on social media, and need an easy to use solution.
  • And lastly, people who make funny cards and want likes.

    • Overview of Routes and End-points

    ConGratZ uses a series of routes and end-points to deliver content to users as follows:

    • The CardViewer
    • Login
    • Register
    • Logout
